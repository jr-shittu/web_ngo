Who we are

Nyawah - Yainkain foundation

The Nyawah - Yainkain foundation is a platform to amplify the voices of women on issues posing serious challenges to the existence of women on one hand and an outlet to shape the future of women and girls in a male dominated world. We are committed to giving every underprivileged woman and young girl an opportunity to thrive in the areas of education, entrepreneurship, health, equality and advocate for representation at the peace table. We tend to take the bottom top approach by working in the spaces deemed as poor, indigent, unsuitable and uncultured, helping women and young girls tackle societal barriers and supporting them to create empowering lives for themselves, their families and their communities. Our work with Women at the top tends to focus on advocacy teaching and training (AT&T) them to be effective in their domain. We are a fully registered entity and recognized by the government of Sierra Leone.

Our Mission

The effective application of deep-seated advocacy and the efficient management of resources to create the change women need.

Our Vision

We are leveraging the manifold opportunities for women in our lifetime, multiply their influence and voices, and set the agenda for their economic freedom.

Core Values

Hope, Courage, Respect, Responsibility and Accountability.
